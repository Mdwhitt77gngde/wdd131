Site Plan

Site Name: Finance Social

Finance Social is a platform that combines personal finance tracking with social media features, providing users with tools to manage their finances effectively while connecting with a community of like-minded individuals.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for users to track their personal finances, share financial tips, and engage in discussions about financial topics.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: How can users track their monthly expenses?
  • Scenario 2: How can users share their financial tips with the community?
